Common Questions

What are the school timings? expand_more
Our regular school hours are from 8:30 AM to 1:30 PM. We also offer extended daycare facilities until 6:00 PM for working parents.
Is food provided at school? expand_more
Yes, we provide a balanced, nutritionist-approved mid-day meal and snacks. We cater to all dietary restrictions and maintain strict hygiene standards in our kitchen.
How safe is the campus? expand_more
Safety is our top priority. We have full CCTV coverage, verified security staff, and all corners are child-proofed. Every visitor is strictly logged and authorized.
